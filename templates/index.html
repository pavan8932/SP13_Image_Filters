📖 Understanding SP13: Smoothing vs Sharpening

  • Mean Filter (Smoothing): Averages neighboring pixels → Reduces noise, creates blur
  • Gaussian Filter (Smoothing): Weighted average (center matters more) → Natural blur, better edge preservation
  • Laplacian Filter (Sharpening): Detects edges using second derivative → Enhances details, makes image sharper
  • Median Filter: Takes median of neighbors → Removes salt-and-pepper noise
  • Bilateral Filter: Edge-preserving smoothing → Blurs while keeping edges sharp
